# TikTok Events API

The **TikTok Events API** allows you to share marketing data from your server directly to TikTok. This integration provides a more reliable connection than the browser-side Pixel alone.

---

## 🔑 Prerequisites

You will need:
1.  A **TikTok For Business** account.
2.  A **TikTok Pixel ID**.
3.  An **Access Token** (Generated in TikTok Ads Manager).

---

## 🛠 Setup Instructions

### 1. Obtain your Access Token
1. Log in to **TikTok Ads Manager**.
2. Go to **Assets** > **Events**.
3. Click **Manage** under Web Events.
4. Select your Pixel.
5. Go to the **Settings** tab.
6. Scroll to **Events API** and click **Generate Access Token**.

### 2. Configure Eventrix
1. Navigate to your **Project Settings** in the Eventrix Dashboard.
2. Under the **Destinations** tab, find **TikTok Events API**.
3. Paste your **Pixel ID** and **Access Token**.
4. Click **Save Configuration**.

---

## 💎 Benefits of TikTok Events API

-   **Reduced Data Loss**: Capture conversions that would otherwise be missed due to ad-blockers or connection issues.
-   **Enriched Data**: Send additional customer signals to improve TikTok's algorithm and lower your CPA.
-   **Secure Transmission**: Encrypted server-to-server communication ensures your data remains private and compliant.

---

## 🧪 Testing

Use the **TikTok Pixel Helper** and the **Event Monitoring** tab in TikTok Ads Manager to verify that events are reaching TikTok's servers.
